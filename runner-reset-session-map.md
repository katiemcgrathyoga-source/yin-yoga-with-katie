# The Runner's Reset — Production Logistics

> **The session sequences now live in `runner-reset-course-plan.md`** (the
> authoritative course design). This file covers delivery + logistics only.

## What to film

**12 sessions, filmed fresh** — two per week over six weeks:
- 6 × **Post-Run Reset** (short, ~10–15 min)
- 6 × **Rest-Day Deep Stretch** (long, ~30–42 min)

(No pre-run sessions — long passive Yin before a run can blunt performance; Yin
belongs after a run and on rest days.) Full pose sequences, holds and safety cues
for each are in `runner-reset-course-plan.md`.

**Film order:** shoot the six Rest-Day deep sessions first — they contain nearly
every pose; the six Post-Run shorts are subsets from the same block.

## Delivery — minimal-infra (cash first, infra second)

- Host the 12 sessions + bonus videos **unlisted on YouTube** (same as the free
  retreat) — no self-hosting yet.
- Sell via **Gumroad or a Stripe payment link**; on purchase, deliver a private
  page/PDF listing the unlisted links + the scheduler. **No accounts system needed.**
- If/when it validates, fold it into the membership + real player (phase 3).
