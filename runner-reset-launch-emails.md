who took the free Post-Run Reset). Send this once the list has built up for a few
weeks. Katie's voice, with a launch copywriter's structure.

## Sending notes

- **Timing:** E0 (−2 days), E1 (open, day 1 AM), E2 (day 2), E3 (day 3), E4 (day 4),
  E5 (day 5 AM), E6 (day 5 PM / close). Expect the biggest share of sales in the
  final 24 hours.
- **Suppress buyers:** move anyone who purchases into a "bought" segment so they
  stop getting the sell emails (MailerLite: trigger on purchase / a thank-you tag).
- **Two segments later:** the sharp vs. warm split you're testing on the sales page
  can inform subject lines here too — sharper for cold, warmer for this list.
- **After close:** send a short "cart closed — here's what's next / join the
  waitlist" email; re-open each race season (Spring Marathon Prep, Fall PR Push).
