why: >
  Every run is repeated impact your spine absorbs, and the tight hip flexors running
  builds pull the pelvis forward, loading the lower back even more. Gentle movement in
  every direction — folding, extending, twisting, side-bending — helps the back
  recover, and the stillness calms a system that's been braced all run.
angle: "Every run quietly compresses your spine. This is how you undo it."
intro: >
  A gentle reset for the whole spine after the compression of a run — a little
  rounding, a little extending, a twist and a side-bend. Keep everything soft and slow.

  - pose: "sphinx"
    seconds: 120
    sides: 1

  - pose: "childs-pose"
    seconds: 120

  - pose: "banana"
