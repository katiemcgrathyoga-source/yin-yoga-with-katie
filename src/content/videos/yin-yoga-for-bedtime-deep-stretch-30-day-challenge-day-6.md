## What this Yin class is

A deep-stretch evening of Yin to close the day — a nice full-body practice that opens the hips and groin, lengthens the hamstrings and spine, then softens the neck and shoulders before bed. We hold each shape for a few minutes so the tension you've gathered can drain away and your body can settle toward sleep. Come as you are — every pose has a gentler option and a deeper one, and you choose what feels right tonight.

## Who this class is for

Anyone who wants to unwind and stretch a little more before bed — whether you're brand new to Yin or already have a practice. Frog Pose takes us a touch deeper into the hips, so if it feels too intense you're always welcome to soften back into a wide-leg Child's Pose. Wherever you are tonight, this class meets you there.

## What you need

Just your mat, and somewhere warm and quiet where you can be still. A cushion or yoga block nearby is lovely to sit on or rest your forehead against. Dim the lights, and press pause any time you need to.

## How Yin Yoga works — three simple cues

- **Find your edge.** Come to about 80% of your capacity — a real stretch, but never forcing or straining. Softer is perfect before bed.
- **Hold for time.** Stay a few minutes and let the shape do the work. My hold times are only suggestions — come out sooner if you need to.
- **Be still.** Let the body and mind settle, and rest your attention on your breath as it slows.
