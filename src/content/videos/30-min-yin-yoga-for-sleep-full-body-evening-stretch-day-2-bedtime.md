## What this Yin class is

A gentle half-hour of Yin to close the day — slow, deep stretches held for a few minutes each to release the tension you've gathered and settle your body toward sleep. Do it in dim light, right before bed. Come as you are — every pose has a softer option and a deeper one, and you choose what feels right tonight.

## Who this class is for

Anyone who wants to switch off at the end of the day — whether you're new to Yin or already have a practice. If you feel wound up, tired, or restless before bed, this class meets you where you are.

## What you need

Just your mat, and somewhere warm and quiet where you can be still. A cushion or blanket nearby is lovely for extra comfort. Press pause any time you need to.

## How Yin Yoga works — three simple cues

- **Find your edge.** Come to about 80% — a real stretch, but never forcing or straining. Softer is perfect before bed.
- **Hold for time.** Stay a few minutes and let the shape do the work. Come out sooner if you need to.
- **Be still.** Let the body and mind settle, and follow your breath as it slows.
