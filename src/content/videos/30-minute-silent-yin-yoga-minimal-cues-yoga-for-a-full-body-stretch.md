## What this Yin class is

A quiet half-hour of Yin to shut the world out — a simple full-body stretch with minimal cues, where I name each pose and how long we'll stay, then let you sink into the silence. No props, nowhere to be. A lovely reset when you only have half an hour. Come as you are — every pose has a gentler option and a deeper one.

## Who this class is for

Anyone who wants a short, peaceful stretch with very little talking — before bed, after a long day, or any time you need to drop out of your head. New to Yin? Read the pose notes below first, then follow along at your own pace.

## What you need

Just your mat, and a warm, quiet space where you can be still. No props required. Press pause whenever you need to.

## How Yin Yoga works — three simple cues

- **Find your edge.** Come to about 80% of your capacity — a real stretch, but never forcing or straining.
- **Hold for time.** Stay a few minutes and let the shape do the work. Come out sooner if you need to.
- **Be still.** Settle body and mind, and rest your attention on your breath.
